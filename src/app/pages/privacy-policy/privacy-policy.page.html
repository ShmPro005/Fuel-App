Privacy Policy

Fuel Mitra App

1. Information We Collect

To enhance user experience and provide personalized features, Fuel App may collect the following information:

  • Username: Used to personalize the app interface and user data.
  • Email Address: Used for login, support, and optional communication such as app updates or reminders.
  • Phone Number: May be used for identity verification or user support purposes.

We do not collect any other personal or sensitive data beyond the information listed above.

2. Local Data Storage

Fuel App securely stores your fuel records directly on your device:

  • No fuel data is uploaded or stored on external servers unless explicitly backed up by the user.
  • You have full control over your saved fuel calculations and history.
  • Your data remains private and is not shared with third parties.

3. Data Processed Within the App

- The app allows you to input fuel cost, mileage, and consumption data.
- All calculations are performed locally, without requiring internet access.
- Your fuel logs and trip history are never transmitted without your consent.

4. Permissions We Request

To provide essential features, Fuel App may request the following permissions:

  • Storage Access: To save and retrieve fuel cost history locally.
  • Internet Access: For authentication, app updates, or displaying non-intrusive ads (if applicable).

These permissions are used strictly for functionality. We do not misuse or sell your data.

5. Ads and Analytics

- The app may show third-party ads to support free usage.
- No personal data like username, email, or phone number is shared with advertisers.
- We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and usability.

6. Your Data Security

- We take your privacy seriously and follow best practices for data handling.
- Your username, email, and phone number are securely stored and never shared without your consent.
